    Our Vision

    Create value in a global marketplace where anyone can buy & sell

    Everyday our society creates new goods and throws away the old ones. We believe that goods once served its purpose for someone could still be valuable to someone else in a different place and a different situation. At Mercari, we are committed to building a peer-to-peer marketplace where anyone can buy & sell easily and safely, to reduce the waste by finding value in goods that are once loved.
